## **What is this?**

This is a collection of standard operating procedures (SOPs) and defensive strategies for graduate students, postdocs, and junior researchers. It is designed to protect your **Intellectual Property (IP)**, ensure **Data Continuity**, and maintain your **Professional Sovereignty** in an environment that often treats you as a temporary resource.

This guide is not anti-science; it is pro-scientist. It leverages the tools of Open Science‚Äîreproducibility, transparency, and version control‚Äîto create a structural defense against ambiguity and exploitation.
